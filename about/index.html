Welcome To 8Bit Games

Hey everyone! 8Bit Games is a used video game store located in Warren, MI just north of Detroit. We specialize in hard to find and rare games, but also cater to ANYONE looking
for something fun to play! We carry Atari, NES, Genesis, PS4, Xbox One, and everything in between!




About Us!

8Bit Games is an independently owned and operated used video game store located in Warren, MI! (Suburb of Metro Detroit) The owner John has worked video game retail for over 10 years in the area and thrives to offer the largest selection of rare games, and the best customer service of any other competitor.
8Bit Games does not sell ANYTHING on ebay or Amazon or any other online resource. All of our rarest games are kept here in the store for YOU to buy!
Lastly John is a game collector himself, who understands not only the game industry as a whole, but also the collector business and he can not only share his knowledge but also a story or two about his own personal collection!
